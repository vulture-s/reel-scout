Fonts bundled with reel-scout
=============================

All three faces are licensed under the SIL Open Font License 1.1 (OFL), which
permits bundling and embedding in documents and applications.

  Archivo Black   — Omnibus-Type            https://fonts.google.com/specimen/Archivo+Black
  Inter           — Rasmus Andersson        https://fonts.google.com/specimen/Inter
  JetBrains Mono  — JetBrains               https://fonts.google.com/specimen/JetBrains+Mono

These are the Google Fonts `latin` unicode-range slices (already subset to Latin
+ punctuation + digits), retrieved from fonts.gstatic.com.

Traditional/Simplified Chinese is NOT bundled here: Noto Sans TC is ~11 MB and
the glyphs actually needed depend on the content being exported, so a CJK subset
is generated per-export from that export's own text (see theme.cjk_subset).

Full OFL text: https://openfontlicense.org
